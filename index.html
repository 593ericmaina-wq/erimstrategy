    Core analysis

    What's happening under the hood

    A blend of trend, momentum, and structural analysis — the same building blocks professional traders rely on.

    ◒

    Momentum reading

    RSI tracks how stretched a move is, flagging when a pair looks overbought or oversold before the crowd catches on.

    ↕

    Volatility sizing

    Average True Range measures how much a pair typically moves, so stops and targets are set to the market's own rhythm, not a fixed number.

    〜

    Trend direction

    Moving averages establish whether price is riding above or below its recent average — the backbone of every directional call.

    ≈

    Trend strength

    MACD adds a second, independent read on momentum shifts, catching turns that a simple average alone can miss.

    ▭

    Mean reversion

    Bollinger Bands highlight when price has stretched unusually far from its average, a common setup for a snap-back.

    ⌁

    Market structure

    Swing highs and lows are mapped automatically to classify the market as trending or ranging, with break-of-structure alerts.

    Discipline

    Six checks before any signal counts

    A promising score isn't enough on its own. Every potential trade is tested against six independent conditions.

    ✓
    Reward outweighs riskTarget distance comfortably exceeds stop distance
    ✓
    Timeframes alignShort, medium and longer-term bias agree with the call
    ✓
    Structure supports itTrend direction matches the signal, not fighting it
    ✓
    Momentum isn't exhaustedRSI isn't already at an extreme against the trade
    ✓
    No major news in the windowHigh-impact economic events are avoided or flagged
    ✓
    MACD agreesSecondary momentum reading confirms the same direction

    5 or more passes → trade eligible · 3–4 → caution · fewer → the signal is downgraded to a skip, regardless of score.

    Execution

    A complete plan, not just a direction

    Once a setup clears the gate, ERICAnalyzer builds the full plan around it.

    ◎

    Entry, stop & targets

    Entry, stop-loss, and two take-profit levels are set from real support and resistance zones and the pair's current volatility — never arbitrary round numbers.

    ⚖

    Position sizing

    Enter your account size and risk tolerance, and the exact lot size is calculated automatically to keep every trade within your comfort zone.

    ✦

    AI-assisted reasoning

    Claude reviews the full technical picture and recent price action to sharpen levels and explain, in plain language, why a setup looks the way it does.

    ▤

    Trade journal

    Log trades as you take them and track your win rate over time, right alongside the analysis that produced each call.
